# ProductivityHub - Brainstorm Document

## 🧠 Initial Concept

The goal was to create an all-in-one productivity app that brings together everything a student or professional needs to stay organized and focused — all within a single iOS app.  

Instead of juggling multiple apps (to-do lists, timers, music players, calendars), **ProductivityHub** integrates them into one seamless experience.

---

## 💡 Inspiration

Many productivity apps focus on *just one* feature:
- Todoist → tasks  
- Forest → focus timer  
- Spotify → focus music  
- Google Calendar → scheduling  

**ProductivityHub** aims to combine these features simply, visually, and efficiently for people who want to boost daily focus and consistency.

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- College students balancing classes, projects, and personal goals.  
- Remote workers managing deadlines and focus blocks.  
- Anyone wanting to reduce distractions and stay consistent with productivity habits.

---

## 🪜 Key Goals

1. Help users **plan** their day with tasks and a calendar.  
2. Help users **focus** using Pomodoro timers and music.  
3. Encourage **balance** by rewarding breaks and mindful habits.  
4. Create a **clean, minimalist UI** that feels motivating and distraction-free.

---

## 🚀 Core Features (MVP)

| Feature | Description |
|----------|--------------|
| 🗒 Tasks | Add, edit, delete, and mark tasks as complete |
| 🗓 Calendar | View upcoming tasks and schedule events |
| ⏱ Pomodoro Timer | Focus timer with start/pause/reset and progress ring |
| 🎧 Focus Music | Built-in ambient sounds: Rain, Forest, Café, and Lo-Fi |

---

## 🌱 Stretch Features (Future Updates)

- Habit tracker with GitHub-style streaks  
- Weekly productivity analytics  
- Push notifications (hydration reminders, session alerts)  
- Custom sound uploads for Focus Music  
- iCloud or Firebase syncing between devices  

---

## 🎨 Design Inspiration

The visual theme focuses on:
- Calm, minimalist interfaces  
- Light colors for clarity and focus  
- Rounded cards and icons for modern iOS feel  

Font: **San Francisco Rounded**  
Color Palette: **Soft whites, muted grays, accent orange (#F5A623)**  

---

## 🧭 App Flow

1. User opens the app and lands on the **Tasks** tab.  
2. User can swipe to the **Pomodoro** tab to start a focus session.  
3. User can swipe further to reach **Focus Music** to play ambient sounds.  
4. The **Calendar** tab helps users visualize their schedule.  
5. All features are unified under a tab bar for quick access.

---

## 🏁 Vision Statement

> “Your focus, your flow — all in one place.”

ProductivityHub’s mission is to empower users to build productive, mindful routines by blending structure (tasks, calendar) with focus (music, timers).
